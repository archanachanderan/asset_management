Our Products

We manufacture a wide variety of metal caps and precision components to meet different sizes, applications, and industry requirements. Our products are made using high-quality materials and modern production methods to ensure consistent performance and durability.

Our range includes Insert Caps, Step Caps, Bore Caps, and Bypass Caps, each designed to perform a specific role. Whether you need small components for compact spaces or larger parts for heavy-duty use, we have solutions to match your needs.

We manufacture components in many different sizes, starting at just 6 mm in diameter and going up to over 100 mm. This wide size range makes our products suitable for various industries and equipment types.
